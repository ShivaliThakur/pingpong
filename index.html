Status:

Instructions

  1. Move your wrist to move your paddle
  2. The computer is playing on the other side
  3. If you hit the ball you get a point
  4. If you miss the ball the computer gets a point
  5. If the computer gets 3 points you lose and the game stops
  6. Click on the start button to start the game
